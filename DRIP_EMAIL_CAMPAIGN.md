## VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

**What This Campaign Is:**
- Invitation-only (exclusive, not spammy)
- Value-first (benefits, not features)
- Founder-to-founder (peer to peer, not sales)
- Data-backed (confidence without hype)

**What This Campaign Is NOT:**
- Hard sales pitch
- Discount-driven urgency
- Hype without substance
- Overuse of exclamation points or emojis

**Key Messaging Pillars:**
1. **Problem Recognition:** "You already know this is broken."
2. **Vision Alignment:** "We're building the future together."
3. **Competitive Advantage:** "First-mover advantage matters here."
4. **Peer Validation:** "Other programs are already seeing results."
5. **Founder Connection:** "This matters to me personally."
